Package.json: contains packages for an app 
   - "~" vs "^" difference: 
     ~version: only accpets new patch versions
        example: the 1.2.0 will update all future  as 1.2.1 , 1.2.3 , 1.2.x
     ^version:  accept new minor and patch versions

packagelock.json: keeps track of exact version of packages installed
